About Untamed Destinations

Our mission is to uncover Maharashtra's hidden gems and promote sustainable local tourism.

Who We Are

Untamed Destinations was founded with a vision to explore the unexplored. Our focus is on lesser-known locations in Maharashtra, places that offer a unique experience away from commercialized tourism. We work with local trekking groups to bring you closer to nature, history, and adventure.

What We Do

We provide curated travel experiences, focusing on destinations such as Kalu Waterfall, Karla Caves, and Lonavala Lake. Our platform connects you with local trekking groups and offers detailed itineraries, travel tips, and personal stories from explorers like you. We believe in responsible tourism that preserves the natural beauty of these hidden places.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support local communities and promote Maharashtra's natural beauty through sustainable tourism. Whether you are an adventurer, a history enthusiast, or simply looking for a peaceful retreat, we are here to guide you in discovering Maharashtra's best-kept secrets.
